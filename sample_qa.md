# Sample Q&A — HDFC MF FAQ Chatbot (Groww)

These are representative queries with the assistant's expected responses and citations.
Last updated from sources: March 2025.

---

### Q1: What is the expense ratio of HDFC Top 100 Fund?

**A:** HDFC Top 100 Fund has an expense ratio of approximately 0.61% per annum for the Direct Plan and approximately 1.58% per annum for the Regular Plan, as of the latest factsheet. Expense ratios are subject to change; verify the current figure at the official factsheet.  
**Source:** https://www.hdfcfund.com/our-products/equities/hdfc-top-100-fund/scheme-details

---

### Q2: What is the exit load for HDFC Flexi Cap Fund?

**A:** HDFC Flexi Cap Fund charges an exit load of 1% if units are redeemed within 1 year from the date of allotment. No exit load is applicable if redeemed after 1 year.  
**Source:** https://www.hdfcfund.com/our-products/equities/hdfc-flexi-cap-fund

---

### Q3: What is the minimum SIP amount for HDFC Mid-Cap Opportunities Fund on Groww?

**A:** The minimum SIP amount for HDFC Mid-Cap Opportunities Fund on Groww is ₹100 per month. The minimum lump-sum investment is also ₹100.  
**Source:** https://groww.in/mutual-funds/hdfc-mid-cap-opportunities-fund-direct-plan-growth-option

---

### Q4: What is the riskometer level of HDFC Flexi Cap Fund?

**A:** HDFC Flexi Cap Fund is classified as 'Very High Risk' on the SEBI riskometer. This indicates that the fund carries high volatility risk and is suitable for investors with a long investment horizon who are comfortable with significant fluctuations in value.  
**Source:** https://www.amfiindia.com/investor-corner/knowledge-center/riskometer.html

---

### Q5: What is the benchmark of HDFC Top 100 Fund?

**A:** HDFC Top 100 Fund is benchmarked against the NIFTY 100 Total Return Index (TRI). HDFC Flexi Cap Fund uses NIFTY 500 TRI, and HDFC Mid-Cap Opportunities Fund uses NIFTY Midcap 150 TRI.  
**Source:** https://www.hdfcfund.com/our-products/equities

---

### Q6: How do I download my capital gains statement on Groww?

**A:** To download your Capital Gains Statement on Groww: log in to your Groww account, go to 'Reports', select 'Capital Gains', choose the financial year, and download the PDF. This statement is useful for filing your income tax return.  
**Source:** https://groww.in/blog/how-to-download-capital-gains-statement-on-groww

---

### Q7: Is there a lock-in period for HDFC Mid-Cap Opportunities Fund?

**A:** No, HDFC Mid-Cap Opportunities Fund does not have a lock-in period. You can redeem your units at any time, though an exit load of 1% applies if redeemed within 1 year of allotment.  
**Source:** https://groww.in/mutual-funds/hdfc-mid-cap-opportunities-fund-direct-plan-growth-option

### Q10: Who is the fund manager of HDFC Mid-Cap Opportunities Fund?

**A:** HDFC Mid-Cap Opportunities Fund is managed by Chirag Setalvad. The fund was launched on June 25, 2007, and is categorized as a Mid Cap Fund under SEBI's mutual fund categorization norms.
